About Us

Ibis Builders is a residential remodeling company based in Pueblo West Colorado. Ibis Builders was founded in 1996 by Mark Shipe, who graduated from Colorado State University's construction management program in 1988. Our primary focus is the renovation and modernization of of existing homes. We specialize in creating beautiful kitchens, bathrooms, and living spaces including finished basements, staircases and general-finish carpentry.
